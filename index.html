AN INNOVATIVE PAGAN COMMUNITY

WitchesWorkshop has been the first point of contact for many Australian witches and pagans since 1999. In a country as large as Australia, providing an accessible and open online community can be a challenge, but our inclusive approach has made WitchesWorkshop one of the most interesting and successful Facebook pagan groups available.
